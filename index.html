Peer Hackers

In our minds, what seperates the greatest higher learning centers in the world from the rest is a community of people willing to communicate and learn from each other. Peer Hackers is a student organization osted at Wayne State University. We are striving to create a community on our campus that allows the students to:

  • Teach each other
  • Organize workshops and events
  • Pursue innovative thoughts
  • Share and acquire resources
  • Organize and convolute needs to the university
  • Hack stuff
